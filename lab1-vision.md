Project vision statement
The problem within Norfolk region NHS currently is that the missed out-patient is rising this is due to multiple factors such as staff not having the right tools to manage appointments and limited budgets causing staff to overwork and communication failing due to stress. The main stakeholders in this problem is Staff as they are the main driver for appointments and if they can't manage them properly this causes delays, Patients and the government.
The new systems aims to lower amount of missed appointments by , Lowering wasted resources, lowering wasted time for staff and making communication clear between staff while stil being secure this protects the main stakeholder because data leaks could cause lawsuits against the NHS, We also aim to ensure that disabilod people can also access the system and book appointments and since the system also requires that patients create accounts we also need to comply with GDPR.
